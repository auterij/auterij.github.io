—John Drummond, CBS 2 Chicago and author of Thirty Years in the Trenches and It Ain’t Pretty but it’s Real

—Robert J. Herguth, former reporter and columnist with the Chicago Daily News and Chicago Sun-Times

—Jack Coombe, Historian and Novelist
